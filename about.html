About Us

This company has very humble beginnings. We started in 2022 when Taylor King came back from a music festival and was amazed by how compliments he received on his hat that had the word "HAT" stitched into the front. He came to Conner King, his brother, with the idea to create a website that would allow anyone to get their hands on the infamous "HAT" hat.
